Our Activities

Dog Walks

This is a regular activity where the members gather and walk their pet dogs. These activities help keep dogs fit and strengthen the bond between owner and pet.

Dog Training

Dog training sessions are organized to help owners teach their dogs good behavior. This can include basic training such as sitting, lying down, or advanced training such as agility.

Community Services

Dog communities often engage in charitable activities such as visiting nursing homes with their dogs, collecting donations for dog shelters, or participating in dog adoption events.

Seminars and Workshops

Organized to provide education to dog owners on topics such as dog care, behavior, nutrition, or health.
